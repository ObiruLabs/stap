Dear Olivia,

Thank you for your generous gift of $25!

Your donation allows us to help people like Brian. Just a few months ago, Brian called us. He was overwhelmed, frightened and very sick. He had just been diagnosed with HIV and his viral load was off the charts. Brian owns a small business and when he can’t work, he doesn’t get paid. Bills pilling up, sick, newly diagnosed – he was overwhelmed. Thanks to you, we were able to help Brian with emotional, medical and financial support. The good news is Brian is complying with treatment recommendations, his viral load is undetectable and he has happily returned to work.

From 2002 to 2010 newly diagnosed cases of HIV/AIDS in New York State fell by 37%. Your investment in our prevention efforts has made a difference.

Every day brings reasons to be grateful and blessings to be counted. Thank you for being one of them.



Sincerely,
Mary and the rest of the STAP team
